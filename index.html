About us

We are thrilled to announce #Megathon2020, a global 100% virtual event that aims to bring together developers, startup enthusiasts, and aspiring entrepreneurs to share and create solutions that cater to the changing industry requirements. #Megathon2020 will include a #StartupSummit for all the budding entrepreneurs. You are required to share your idea with us, which will be thoroughly analyzed by top-notch industry experts. If your business idea is selected as the 1st, 2nd, or 3rd choice, you will receive monetary rewards from "First Money".

Domain

Virtual and Augmented Reality

Build immersive experiences that enable users to interact with the virtual world or enhance their real-world experiences.

Sustainability

Create solutions that promote sustainable practices such as energy conservation, recycling, and reducing carbon footprint.

Blockchain

Build decentralized applications using blockchain technology for secure transactions and smart contracts.

Artificial Intelligence

Develop solutions that use AI to solve complex problems, such as speech recognition, image processing, or natural language processing.

Education and learning

Develop solutions that improve the quality of education and learning outcomes, such as personalized learning, online learning platforms, gamification, and accessibility.

Agriculture and Food Security

Develop solutions that promote sustainable agriculture and improve food security, such as precision farming, food traceability, and crop management tools.

Financial Inclusion and Economic Empowerment

Develop solutions that promote financial inclusion and economic empowerment, such as improving access to financial services, promoting entrepreneurship, and reducing economic disparities.

Smart Cities and Infrastructure

Develop solutions that improve the quality of life in urban areas, such as smart transportation systems, intelligent energy management, and smart building technologies.

Social Impact

Develop solutions that address social issues like homelessness, hunger and healthcare.
